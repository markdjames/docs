# Contact Details Form

Use this form to update your contact information. Please follow these instructions:

1. **Email**: Enter your email address. This field is required.
2. **Mobile**: Enter your mobile phone number. This field is required.
3. **Home Phone**: Enter your home phone number (optional).

Click **Save** to submit your changes. A success message will appear if your details are updated. If there is an error, an error message will be shown.
