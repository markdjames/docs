# Education Contacts

The Education Contacts form allows admin users to manage the list of education contacts associated with schools or institutions. Use this form to add, edit, or remove education contact records.

## Purpose
This form is designed for admin users to:
- Add new education contacts
- Edit existing education contact details
- Remove education contacts when necessary

## How to Use the Education Contacts Form

1. **View Education Contacts**: The form displays a list of current education contacts, including their names, job titles, and affiliated institutions.
2. **Add Education Contact**:
   - Click the **Add Education Contact** button.
   - Fill in the required fields (see below).
   - Click **Save** to add the new contact.
3. **Edit Education Contact**:
   - Select a contact from the list.
   - Update their details as needed.
   - Click **Save** to apply changes.
4. **Remove Education Contact**:
   - Select the contact to remove.
   - Click the **Delete** or **Remove** button.
   - Confirm the action when prompted.

## Key Fields
| Field                     | Description                                                                 |
|---------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Title**                  | Contact's title (e.g., Mr, Ms, Dr).                                       |
| **Firstname**              | Contact's first name (required).                                           |
| **Surname**                | Contact's surname (required).                                             |
| **Job Title**              | Contact's job title (required).                                           |
| **Email**                  | Contact's email address (required).                                       |
| **Affiliated Institution** | The school or institution the contact is associated with (dropdown).      |
