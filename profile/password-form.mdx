---
title: Password Form
---

Use this form to update your account password. Please follow these instructions:

1. **Current password**: Enter your current password. This field is required.
2. **New password**: Enter your new password. This field is required. Your new password must:
   - Be exactly 8 characters long
   - Not have more than 2 identical characters in a row
   - Contain at least one special character (!@#$%^&*)
   - Contain at least one lowercase letter (a-z)
   - Contain at least one uppercase letter (A-Z)
   - Contain at least one number (0-9)
3. **Confirm new password**: Re-enter your new password to confirm. This field is required.

If your new password does not meet the requirements or the passwords do not match, you will see a warning. When all requirements are met, you will see a success message.

Click **Update** to submit your new password. A success message will confirm the update. If there is an error, an error message will be displayed.
